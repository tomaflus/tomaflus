## About

I'm the founder of **Dervora Digital**, a full-service digital agency based in Bosnia and Herzegovina. We handle the full stack of digital work — UI/UX, web development, native Android apps, AI/ML integration, SEO, and performance marketing.

I also co-founded **Customer2Go**, building consumer-facing mobile and web products aimed at regional markets.

My focus is on shipping clean, production-ready software: nothing over-engineered, nothing half-finished.
